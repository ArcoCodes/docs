## More than just email

Gmail integration is powerful, but it's not the only way to work with Kairos. Your AI manager understands multiple input methods — so you can interact however is most natural for you.

## Ways to interact

### Direct messages

The simplest way: just type what you need in Telegram.

- _"I got a deal offer from Nike for 2 Instagram reels at $3,000"_
- _"Schedule a call with my editor tomorrow at 2 PM"_
- _"What's on my calendar this week?"_
- _"Research a brand called FitBrand Co"_

Kairos understands natural language and figures out what you need from context.

### Screenshots

Can't copy text or forward a message? Take a screenshot and send it. Kairos reads screenshots and extracts the relevant information:

- **Brand deal DMs** from Instagram, X, TikTok, or any platform
- **Social media profiles** for creator profile setup or updates
- **Contract pages** for quick review
- **Payment confirmations** or invoices
- **Campaign briefs** or brand guidelines

Both light and dark mode screenshots work fine. If content spans multiple screens, send multiple screenshots — Kairos combines them.

### Forward messages

Got a brand deal in your Telegram DMs? Just forward the message to your Kairos bot. It picks up the sender info and content automatically.

For DMs on other platforms, copy the message text or take a screenshot and send it to your bot. Adding a brief note like _"This is a brand deal offer from Instagram"_ helps, but Kairos can usually figure out the context on its own.

### Copy and paste

You can also paste text directly into Telegram — email content from a webmail client, deal terms from a document, brand briefs from any source.

## When to use what

| Method | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Gmail integration** | Automatic monitoring — deals come to you without lifting a finger |
| **Screenshots** | DMs, contracts, profiles, anything visual |
| **Forwarded messages** | Telegram conversations, quick message sharing |
| **Copy-paste** | Email content, deal terms, brand briefs |
| **Direct description** | When you want to summarize a deal in your own words |

All methods feed into the same workflow — deal detection, evaluation, negotiation, and beyond.

## No Gmail required

All of these methods work whether or not you have Gmail connected. If you prefer not to connect your email, you can still use the full brand deal workflow by sharing deal information through any of the methods above.

<Info>
  Kairos adapts to how you prefer to work. Some creators use Gmail integration for everything. Others prefer forwarding screenshots. Both approaches work equally well.
</Info>

## Combining methods

You can mix and match freely:

- Use Gmail for automatic deal detection, but forward DM deals manually
- Send screenshots of contracts instead of waiting for the email
- Describe a deal verbally and add a screenshot for the details

Kairos combines information from all sources to build a complete picture.
