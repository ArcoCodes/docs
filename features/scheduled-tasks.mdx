## Automate your routine

Kairos lets you create scheduled tasks that run automatically on a recurring basis. Morning schedule summaries, payment due date checks, weekly content reminders — you decide what, when, and how often.

## How it works

Just tell your AI manager what you want scheduled:

- _"Send me a morning schedule summary every day at 9 AM"_
- _"Remind me to check my inbox every Monday and Thursday at 10 AM"_
- _"Run a payment check every morning"_

Kairos creates the task with the right timing and instructions. You can manage all your tasks through simple conversation.

## Built-in tasks

Kairos comes with a few pre-configured tasks you can activate:

| Task | Default time | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Morning reminder** | 10:00 AM | Sends a summary of today's calendar events |
| **Evening preview** | 10:00 PM | Shows what's on for tomorrow |
| **Payment tracker** | 10:00 AM | Checks for overdue payments in Notion and alerts you |

You can customize the timing, content, or turn any of these on or off.

## Custom tasks

Beyond the built-in options, you can create your own scheduled tasks for anything:

- Weekly content performance summaries
- Bi-weekly brand deal pipeline reviews
- Daily deadline reminders during production phases
- Recurring check-ins on specific topics

Each task has a name, schedule, and instructions that Kairos follows when it runs.

## Managing tasks

You can manage your scheduled tasks through conversation:

- _"Show me my scheduled tasks"_
- _"Pause the morning reminder"_
- _"Change the payment check to 8 AM"_
- _"Delete the weekly summary task"_

<Info>
  You can have up to 5 scheduled tasks running at a time. This keeps things focused and prevents notification overload.
</Info>

## Flexible scheduling

Tasks support flexible timing patterns:

- **Daily** — every day at a specific time
- **Weekly** — specific days of the week
- **Custom** — any recurring pattern you need (every 2 days, first Monday of the month, etc.)

All times are based on your timezone.
