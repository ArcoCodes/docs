## From inbox to income

Brand deals involve a lot of back-and-forth: reading emails, researching companies, negotiating rates, tracking deliverables, chasing payments. Kairos handles this entire workflow so you can focus on creating great content.

Your AI manager can pick up a deal at any stage — whether it starts from an email, a DM you forward, or a deal you describe directly.

## The deal lifecycle

Kairos organizes brand deals into stages. You don't have to use every stage — just the ones that help you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Detection">
    Kairos spots brand deal emails in your inbox (or you can forward/describe them manually). It sends you a quick summary with the brand name, what they're offering, and compensation if mentioned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Evaluation">
    Your AI manager researches the brand, assesses how well it fits your content, suggests a pricing range, and flags any red flags. The full report is saved to Notion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Negotiation">
    Get advice on pricing, deliverables, and timelines. Kairos can draft reply emails and update your Notion deal page as the conversation progresses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contract review">
    When a contract arrives, Kairos highlights key terms like payment terms, exclusivity, usage rights, and kill fees — so you know what to watch out for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Production & delivery">
    Track milestones on your calendar, manage deliverable submissions, and keep everything organized in Notion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment tracking">
    Kairos records payment due dates and checks daily for overdue payments. It can draft professional follow-up emails to help you get paid on time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Everything stays in Notion

If you connect Notion, every deal gets its own page with:

- Brand evaluation report and fit assessment
- Negotiation log (updated as conversations progress)
- Scheduling milestones (synced from Google Calendar)
- Contract review notes
- Deliverable URLs and delivery dates
- Payment records and receipts

Kairos adapts to your existing Notion setup — it won't delete or rename your columns.

<Info>
  **Don't use Notion?** No problem. Kairos still handles detection, evaluation, and email drafting without it. Notion just adds a layer of organization.
</Info>

## You're always in control

- No email is ever sent without your explicit approval
- Every action starts with buttons you can tap — or just type naturally
- You can skip any stage, jump ahead, or ignore a deal entirely
- Your preferences are remembered over time, so the experience gets better the more you use it
