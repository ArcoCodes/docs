## Help your AI manager understand you

Your profile is what Kairos uses to assess brand fit, suggest pricing, and tailor advice to your specific situation. The more it knows about your content and audience, the better its recommendations.

## How to set up your profile

Kairos guides you through the process step by step. There's no form to fill out — just a conversation.

### Share your social media

You can provide your profile information in several ways:

- **Share a URL** — send a link to your YouTube channel, X profile, or other platforms
- **Connect via OAuth** — for TikTok, you can connect your account directly from the dashboard for automatic data sync
- **Send a screenshot** — take a screenshot of your profile page and send it to your bot
- **Describe it** — just tell Kairos about your content, audience, and platforms

Kairos works with any combination of these. Use whatever's easiest for you.

### Supported platforms

Kairos can analyze profiles from YouTube, X/Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and more. TikTok supports direct OAuth connection from your dashboard for the most accurate data. For other platforms, URLs or screenshots work great — Kairos will extract the relevant information automatically.

## What Kairos analyzes

When you share your profile, Kairos builds a comprehensive picture:

| Dimension | What's analyzed |
|---|---|
| **Niche** | Your content category and sub-topics |
| **Engagement** | Recent engagement rates |
| **Content style** | Format preferences, posting patterns, production quality |
| **Tone** | Formality, humor, educational depth, personality |
| **Audience** | Follower counts, growth trends, audience demographics (when available) |

## When is your profile needed?

Your profile is checked at the start of every deal evaluation. If you haven't set one up yet, Kairos will ask you before proceeding — it needs this context to give you accurate fit assessments and pricing suggestions.

<Tip>
  You don't have to set up your profile in advance. Kairos will prompt you when it's needed and walk you through the process.
</Tip>

## Multi-platform support

If you're active on multiple platforms, Kairos combines data from all of them into a single profile. This gives a more complete picture of your reach and content style for evaluations.

## Privacy

Your profile data is stored privately in your sandbox. It's used only for your deal evaluations and is never shared with brands or other users.
