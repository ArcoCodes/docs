## Your profile grows with you

Your follower counts, engagement rates, and content style evolve over time. Kairos keeps your creator profile current so that brand evaluations always reflect where you are now — not where you were months ago.

## Automatic refresh

Kairos checks your profile age before every deal evaluation. If your profile is older than 7 days, it automatically refreshes the data before proceeding. You don't have to think about it.

## Manual refresh

You can also update your profile anytime:

- _"Refresh my profile"_
- _"Update my YouTube stats"_
- _"I just hit 50K on Instagram, can you update my profile?"_

Send new URLs, screenshots, or just tell Kairos what's changed.

## Profile history

Kairos saves snapshots of your profile over time. This means you and your AI manager can:

- Track follower growth across platforms
- See how engagement rates have changed
- Notice shifts in content direction
- Use growth trends as leverage in deal negotiations

<Tip>
  When negotiating a deal, Kairos can reference your growth trajectory — for example, _"Your YouTube channel has grown 20% in the last 3 months"_ — to support a stronger rate.
</Tip>

## What triggers a refresh

| Trigger | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Deal evaluation** | Profile is refreshed if older than 7 days |
| **Your request** | Refreshed immediately with latest data |
| **New platform added** | You share a new profile URL or screenshot, and it's added to your profile |

## Same flexible input

Just like the initial setup, you can update your profile by sharing URLs, screenshots, or descriptions. Kairos handles the rest.
