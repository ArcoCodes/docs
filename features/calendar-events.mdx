## Your schedule, managed for you

Kairos can create and manage Google Calendar events directly from your Telegram conversation. Whether it's a brand shoot, a meeting, or a personal reminder — just tell your AI manager and it handles the rest.

## What you can do

### Create events

Tell Kairos what to schedule in natural language:

- _"Schedule a shoot next Tuesday at 2 PM in downtown LA"_
- _"Add a call with Nike's team on Friday at 10 AM for 30 minutes"_
- _"Block off Saturday afternoon for editing"_

Kairos creates the event with the right time, duration, location, and description. It handles timezone conversion automatically.

### View your schedule

- _"What's on my calendar today?"_
- _"Do I have anything on Thursday?"_
- _"Show me next week's schedule"_

Kairos pulls your events and presents them clearly with times, locations, and attendee info.

### Update events

- _"Move the Nike call to 3 PM"_
- _"Add 30 minutes to tomorrow's editing block"_
- _"Update the location for Friday's shoot"_

Only the fields you mention are changed — everything else stays the same.

## From emails to events

When Kairos detects a schedule-related email (meeting invite, event update), it offers to create a calendar event for you with a single tap. The event details are pulled directly from the email.

## From brand deals to milestones

During deal negotiation, Kairos can create production milestones as calendar events — script review deadlines, first draft delivery, publication dates. These are synced to your Notion deal page for a complete view.

<Info>
  Kairos never creates or modifies calendar events without your confirmation. It always shows you the details first and waits for your approval.
</Info>

## Daily reminders

If you set up scheduled reminders, Kairos can send you:

- **Morning summary** — your day's events at a glance
- **Evening preview** — what's on for tomorrow

These are fully customizable through [scheduled tasks](/features/scheduled-tasks).
