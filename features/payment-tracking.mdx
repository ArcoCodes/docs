## Get paid on time

One of the most frustrating parts of brand deals is waiting for payment — and having to follow up when it's late. Kairos tracks your payment due dates and helps you send professional reminders so you don't have to chase money yourself.

## How it works

### Recording payment terms

After a deal is delivered, Kairos records:

- **Payment due date** — based on the contract terms (Net 30, Net 60, etc.)
- **Deal value** — the final agreed amount
- **Deal status** — set to "Awaiting Payment"

All of this is saved to your Notion deal page (if connected).

### Automatic monitoring (optional)

Kairos can offer to set up a daily payment check as a [scheduled task](/features/scheduled-tasks). You just choose whether to enable it or not. Once enabled, it automatically scans for overdue payments and notifies you. The timing is fully customizable.

If a payment is past due, you'll get a notification with:

- Brand name
- How many days it's overdue
- The expected amount

Along with buttons:
- **Send reminder** — Kairos drafts a professional payment follow-up email
- **Acknowledge** — mark it as noted (no action needed right now)

### Payment confirmation

When the brand confirms payment (via email or you tell Kairos directly), it:

- Updates the deal status to "Paid"
- Records the payment amount and date
- Downloads and stores any receipt or invoice attachments to Notion

## Collection emails

If you choose to send a reminder, Kairos drafts emails with a professional but firm tone:

- First reminder: friendly check-in referencing the original terms
- Subsequent reminders: progressively more direct while staying professional
- Each email includes the invoice reference, original terms, amount due, and days overdue

Every draft is shown to you for review before sending. Kairos learns your preferred approach to collection over time.

<Info>
  Kairos never sends payment reminder emails without your explicit approval.
</Info>

## Notion tracking

Your deal page maintains a complete payment record:

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Payment due date** | When payment is expected |
| **Deal value** | Final agreed amount |
| **Status** | Awaiting Payment → Paid |
| **Payment records** | Amounts, dates, and receipts in a sub-page |
