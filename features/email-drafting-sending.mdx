## Write emails faster

Whether you're replying to a brand, following up on a payment, or sending a cold pitch, Kairos drafts emails that match your tone and style. Nothing is ever sent without your say-so.

## How it works

### Ask for a draft

You can ask Kairos to draft an email in several ways:

- Tap **Reply Directly** on an email notification
- Ask: _"Help me write a follow-up email to Nike about the campaign timeline"_
- Tap **Send Reminder** on a payment notification
- Say: _"Draft a professional decline email to that skincare brand"_

### Review before sending

Every email draft is presented in Telegram with three options:

- **Send** — send the email exactly as shown
- **Edit** — tell Kairos what to change, and it'll revise the draft
- **Cancel** — discard the draft entirely

<Tip>
  You can go through as many edit rounds as you need. Just describe what you want changed: _"Make it shorter"_, _"Sound more enthusiastic"_, _"Add that we're available next week"_.
</Tip>

## Smart context

Kairos drafts aren't generic templates. They're aware of your conversation history, deal context, and your communication style — so every draft feels like something you'd actually write.

## What you can draft

| Use case | Example |
|---|---|
| **Brand reply** | Respond to a collaboration invite with your rates and availability |
| **Negotiation** | Counter-offer on pricing, deliverables, or timeline |
| **Follow-up** | Check in on a deal that's gone quiet |
| **Payment reminder** | Professional follow-up on an overdue invoice |
| **Delivery email** | Submit content with deliverable links |
| **Decline** | Politely pass on a deal that isn't a good fit |

## Threading

When you reply to an email that Kairos detected, the reply is properly threaded in Gmail — it shows up in the same conversation, not as a new email. This keeps your inbox organized and gives the brand full context.

## Privacy and control

- **No email is ever sent without your explicit confirmation**
- Drafts are generated on the fly — they're not stored or logged
- You can always type your own reply instead of using a draft
- Kairos never contacts brands on its own initiative
