## Stay on track from start to finish

Once a deal is confirmed, Kairos helps you manage the production process — from planning your schedule to submitting final deliverables.

## Milestone tracking

Kairos can set up production milestones on your Google Calendar:

- Script review deadline
- First draft delivery
- Revision rounds
- Final delivery
- Publication date

If milestones were discussed during negotiation, Kairos carries them forward. Otherwise, it suggests a timeline based on the contract terms and asks for your confirmation before creating any events.

<Info>
  Calendar events are never created without your approval. Kairos always shows you the plan first.
</Info>

## Deliverable management

As you work through the production process, Kairos can:

- Collect deliverable URLs (script docs, video drafts, final cuts)
- Store them in your Notion deal page under a Deliverables sub-page
- Help draft delivery emails to the brand
- Track what's been submitted and what's still pending

## Notion updates

Your deal page in Notion is updated throughout production:

| What's tracked | Details |
|---|---|
| **Status** | Moves from "In Production" to "Delivered" as you progress |
| **Milestones** | Synced from Google Calendar with dates |
| **Deliverables** | URLs and delivery dates for each submission |

## Delivery assistance

When you're ready to submit, tell your AI manager and it can:

1. Collect the deliverable links you want to include
2. Draft a professional delivery email to the brand
3. Show you the draft for review
4. Send it after your approval
5. Update your Notion page with the delivery record

## What's next

Once delivery is complete, Kairos moves to [payment tracking](/features/payment-tracking) — recording the payment due date and monitoring for overdue payments.
