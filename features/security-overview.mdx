## Your data, your control

We know you're trusting Kairos with sensitive information — emails, brand deals, contracts, financial details. We take that responsibility seriously.

## How we protect your data

### Isolated environments

Every user gets a completely private sandbox environment. Your data, credentials, and AI manager state are isolated from every other user. There's no shared storage or cross-user access.

### Encryption

All credentials (Gmail tokens, Notion tokens, bot tokens) are encrypted with **AES-256-GCM** — the same encryption standard used by banks and financial institutions. Credentials are encrypted at rest and decrypted only when needed for authorized operations.

### Email privacy

When a new email arrives, Gmail sends Kairos a notification with only an ID number — not the email itself. Kairos forwards this ID to your private environment, where your AI manager reads the email directly from Gmail. Our server only stores a short ID for each notification to avoid processing it twice. Email subjects, body text, attachments, and sender information never touch our database.

### Telegram security

Your Telegram bot is created and owned by you through BotFather. Kairos verifies your identity through the bot's owner ID for every action — so nobody else can interact with your AI manager, even in group chats.

## What Kairos can and cannot do

| | Details |
|---|---|
| **Can read** | Emails (Gmail), calendar events, Notion pages you've granted access to |
| **Can create** | Calendar events, Notion pages, email drafts |
| **Can send** | Emails — only after your explicit confirmation |
| **Cannot access** | Google Drive, Google Photos, or any service you haven't connected |
| **Cannot delete** | Calendar events are never deleted without your confirmation |
| **Cannot share** | Your data is never shared with other users, brands, or third parties |

## You're always in control

- **Disconnect anytime** — remove Google, Notion, or any connection from your dashboard whenever you want
- **Delete your bot** — deleting your Telegram bot removes your sandbox and all associated data. Our servers don't store your personal sensitive data.
- **Confirmation required** — no email is sent, no event is created without your approval
- **Transparent actions** — Kairos always tells you what it's doing and why

## Permissions we request

### Google

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| Read emails (gmail.readonly) | Classify and notify you about brand deals and important emails |
| Send emails (gmail.send) | Send replies you've approved |
| Calendar events (calendar.events) | Create and manage your schedule |
| Basic identity (openid, email) | Identify your account |

No access to Drive, Photos, Contacts, or other Google services.

### Notion

Kairos requests access only to the pages and databases you explicitly share during the connection process.

### Telegram

Your bot token is used solely to communicate with you through your personal bot. It's encrypted and stored in your isolated environment.
