## Your inbox, filtered for you

Creators get a lot of email — brand pitches, event invitations, fan messages, newsletters, spam. When a new email arrives, Gmail notifies Kairos instantly, and your AI manager filters out the noise and alerts you about what actually matters: brand deals, schedule-related messages (meeting invites, restaurant reservations, travel itineraries), and more.

You can customize which categories of emails you want to be notified about — just tell your AI manager what matters to you and what doesn't.

## How it works

### Real-time push notifications

When you connect Gmail, Kairos receives push notifications the moment a new email arrives. There's no polling or delay — you're notified in Telegram as soon as something important comes in.

### Smart classification

Not every email deserves your attention. Kairos intelligently filters out the noise — newsletters, promotions, automated receipts — and only surfaces the emails that need your action.

It understands emails in any language, so brand deals in English, Japanese, or any other language are handled the same way.

## What you receive

For each important email, you get a Telegram notification with:

- A concise summary of the email content
- Key details highlighted (dates, amounts, brand names)
- Action buttons relevant to the email type

For brand deal emails, you'll see: **Evaluate** / **Reply Directly** / **Ignore**

For schedule-related emails: **Create Event** / **Ignore**

## Smart filtering

Kairos gets smarter over time:

- If you tell it "skip brand deals that don't mention a budget," it remembers
- Emails from brands you're already working with are linked to the existing deal
- Kairos platform emails (from the Kairos team) are always treated as important

<Info>
  **Gmail not connected?** You can still forward important emails to your bot as text or screenshots. Kairos will read and classify them the same way.
</Info>
