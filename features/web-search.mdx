## Search the web, from Telegram

Kairos has built-in web search — no API keys, no setup, no extra tools. Just ask a question and your AI manager finds the answer.

## How to use it

Ask anything you'd normally search for:

- _"What's the average rate for YouTube sponsorships in the tech niche?"_
- _"When is VidCon 2026?"_
- _"What are the FTC guidelines for sponsored content disclosure?"_
- _"Find me some examples of good brand deal media kits"_

Kairos searches the web and gives you a concise, useful answer — not just a list of links.

## What makes it useful

### No setup required

Web search works out of the box. There's nothing to configure or connect.

### News and time-sensitive queries

Need recent information? Kairos can filter results by time:

- Latest news about a brand
- Recent industry trends
- Current pricing benchmarks
- Upcoming events and dates

### Context-aware

When you search during a deal evaluation or negotiation, Kairos combines web results with your deal context. For example, searching _"average rate for Instagram Reels"_ during a negotiation will factor in your creator tier and the specific deal terms.

## Beyond brand deals

Web search isn't just for deals. You can use it for anything:

- Content research and inspiration
- Industry news and trends
- Fact-checking claims from brands
- Finding tools, templates, or resources
