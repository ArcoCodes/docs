## An AI manager that remembers

<Tip>
  **The more you use Kairos, the better it understands how you work.** It learns your preferences from your actions and applies them automatically — so you spend less time repeating yourself and more time creating.
</Tip>

## What Kairos learns

Kairos picks up on things like:

- Your brand deal preferences and pricing approach
- Your communication style and tone
- What notifications you care about and what you don't
- How you like your schedule organized

## Transparency

Kairos doesn't silently override your decisions. When a stored preference conflicts with the current situation, it'll surface the conflict and let you decide:

_"You typically decline deals from supplement brands, but this one has a high fit score. Would you like me to evaluate it anyway?"_

## Giving instructions

You can set preferences explicitly at any time:

- _"Always use Deep research for deals over $5,000"_
- _"I prefer short, direct emails — no fluff"_
- _"Don't wake me up with notifications before 9 AM"_
- _"Skip any emails from noreply addresses"_

These take the highest priority and override learned patterns.

<Tip>
  You can also update or remove preferences anytime. Just tell Kairos: _"Stop filtering newsletter emails"_ or _"Actually, switch back to Standard research by default."_
</Tip>
