## Know who you're working with

Before you say yes to a brand deal, it helps to know who's behind it. Kairos can research any brand on demand — whether it's part of a deal evaluation or just a quick check because you're curious.

## How to use it

Just ask:

- _"Research FitBrand Co"_
- _"Can you look into this company?"_
- _"What do you know about [brand name]?"_

Or tap **Evaluate** on a deal notification, and brand research runs automatically as part of the evaluation.

## Two research modes

Kairos offers a **Standard** mode for quick checks and a **Deep** mode for thorough investigations. For details on what each mode covers, see [Deal Evaluation → Two research depths](/features/deal-evaluation#two-research-depths).

You can also use brand research independently — you don't have to be in a deal evaluation to research a brand.

## What you get

A factual research report covering:

- Company background and legitimacy
- Product/service reputation with real user sentiment
- Creator collaboration history and track record
- Red flags (if any are found)
- An overall assessment of whether this brand is trustworthy

The report is saved to your Notion deal page (if connected) and summarized in your Telegram chat.
