## Overview

LINE is one of the messaging platforms Kairos supports. Particularly popular in Japan, LINE lets you interact with Kairos right inside the app you already use every day. Kairos sends notifications, receives your messages, and shows interactive action buttons through LINE Flex Messages — all without leaving a single chat thread.

## Setting up

1. Go to your Kairos dashboard and select **LINE** as your messaging platform
2. Scan the QR code to add the Kairos LINE official account, or tap the direct link
3. Open the Kairos account in LINE and send the **pairing code** shown on your dashboard
4. Once the code is verified, Kairos will activate your sandbox environment automatically

The pairing code expires after a few minutes. If it expires, click **Refresh** on your dashboard to generate a new one.

<Tip>
  You can also add the Kairos LINE official account by visiting [https://lin.ee/XSVJ0SY](https://lin.ee/XSVJ0SY) directly.
</Tip>

## What you can do in LINE

Your Kairos LINE account is the single interface for everything Kairos offers:

- **Receive notifications** about new emails, brand deals, schedule updates, and payment reminders
- **Tap action buttons** on Flex Messages to evaluate deals, reply to emails, create events, and more
- **Send messages** in natural language to ask questions, give instructions, or describe deals
- **Forward messages** from other chats for Kairos to process
- **Send screenshots** of DMs, profiles, contracts, or anything you want Kairos to read
- **Set preferences and rules** just by telling Kairos what you want

## Security

Your LINE account is tied to your Kairos account through a verified pairing code process. Only the paired LINE user can interact with Kairos. Messages are processed in an isolated sandbox environment dedicated to your account.

## Disconnecting

If you want to stop using Kairos through LINE, go to your Kairos dashboard and disconnect. This removes your sandbox environment and all associated data.

You can also block the Kairos LINE official account from within LINE. If you later re-add the account and pair again, it will be a completely fresh environment with no previous data.
