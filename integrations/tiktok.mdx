## Overview

Connecting TikTok lets Kairos access your public profile data — followers, engagement rates, and content performance — to provide more accurate brand deal evaluations and profile analysis. It's optional, and you can always share this information manually instead.

## Connecting

1. Go to your Kairos dashboard
2. Click **Connect TikTok**
3. Sign in with your TikTok account
4. Review the permissions and click **Authorize**

## What Kairos accesses

| Data | What it's used for |
|---|---|
| **Public profile info** | Your display name, bio, and profile picture for creator profile context |
| **Follower count** | Used in pricing calculations and brand fit assessments |
| **Engagement metrics** | Likes, comments, shares, and view counts to assess engagement rates |
| **Content performance** | Recent video performance data to identify trends and growth |

Kairos does **not** access your DMs, private or draft content, or post anything on your behalf. It's read-only access to public analytics data.

## How it's used

Once connected, your TikTok data enriches your creator profile automatically:

- **Brand deal evaluations** become more accurate with real engagement data instead of estimates
- **Growth tracking** lets Kairos monitor follower and engagement trends over time
- **Notion sync** — if you've connected Notion, TikTok analytics are included in your synced profile data

For more on how your profile is used in evaluations, see [Profile Setup](/features/profile-setup) and [Deal Evaluation](/features/deal-evaluation).

## Without TikTok

If you prefer not to connect your TikTok account, you can still:

- Send screenshots of your TikTok profile or analytics page
- Share your TikTok profile URL for Kairos to analyze
- Describe your metrics directly in conversation

See [Screenshots, Messages & Forwarding](/features/screenshots-forwarding) for details.

## Privacy

Your TikTok data is stored privately in your sandbox and used only for your evaluations. Kairos has no write access to your account and never shares your data with brands or other users.

## Disconnecting

You can disconnect TikTok from your Kairos dashboard at any time. Once disconnected, Kairos stops pulling analytics data from your account.

To reconnect, just click **Connect TikTok** again and go through the authorization flow.
