## Overview

Connecting Gmail lets Kairos receive notifications when new emails arrive, read them to identify brand deals and schedule items, and send replies you've approved. It's optional, and many features work without it.

## Connecting

1. Go to your Kairos dashboard
2. Click **Connect Google**
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Review the permissions and click **Continue**

## Permissions

| Permission | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Read emails** (gmail.readonly) | Reads new emails to classify and notify you about brand deals, schedule items, and important messages |
| **Send emails** (gmail.send) | Sends replies you've approved. No email is ever sent without your confirmation |
| **Basic identity** (openid, email) | Identifies your account. No access to Drive, Photos, or other Google services |

<Info>
  Gmail and Google Calendar are connected through the same Google sign-in. You don't need to authorize twice.
</Info>

## How it works

When a new email arrives, Gmail sends a notification to Kairos with just an ID number (not the email content). Your AI manager then reads the email directly from Gmail inside your private environment. Our servers never store email content.

For more on what Kairos does with your emails, see [Email Monitoring](/features/email-monitoring) and [Email Drafting & Sending](/features/email-drafting-sending).

## Disconnecting and reconnecting

You can disconnect Gmail from your Kairos dashboard at any time. Once disconnected, Kairos stops receiving email notifications and can no longer read or send emails.

To reconnect, just click **Connect Google** again and go through the authorization flow.

## Without Gmail

If you prefer not to connect Gmail, you can still:

- Forward emails to your bot as screenshots or text
- Describe brand deals directly in conversation
- Create calendar events by telling your bot what to schedule

See [Screenshots, Messages & Forwarding](/features/screenshots-forwarding) for details.
