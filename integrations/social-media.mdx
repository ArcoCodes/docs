## Overview

Kairos uses your social media data to build your creator profile, which powers deal evaluations, fit assessments, and pricing suggestions. You don't need to "connect" your social accounts in the traditional sense. Instead, you share your information in whatever way is easiest.

## How to share your profiles

### Send a URL

The simplest way. Just paste your profile link into the Telegram bot:

> _"Here's my YouTube channel: youtube.com/@yourchannel"_

Kairos fetches your public profile data, recent content, and engagement metrics.

### Send a screenshot

If a URL doesn't work, or you want to share stats that aren't publicly visible, take a screenshot of your profile page and send it to your bot. Kairos reads the image and extracts the numbers.

This works for any platform, including ones without public APIs.

### Describe it

You can also just tell Kairos about your channels:

> _"I have 45K subscribers on YouTube, mostly fitness content. My last 10 videos averaged about 15K views."_

## Supported platforms

Kairos can analyze profiles from **YouTube**, **X/Twitter**, **TikTok**, **Instagram**, and more. If you send a screenshot, virtually any platform works.

## Multi-platform profiles

If you're active on multiple platforms, share all of them. Kairos combines the data into a single creator profile that gives a more complete picture of your reach and content style.

## Keeping your profile updated

Your profile is refreshed automatically when it's older than 7 days and you start a new deal evaluation. You can also update it anytime by sending new URLs or screenshots, or just telling Kairos what's changed.

See [Profile Setup](/features/profile-setup) and [Profile Refresh](/features/profile-refresh) for more details.
