## Overview

Telegram is how you talk to Kairos. You create your own bot through Telegram's BotFather, connect it to your Kairos account, and everything happens through that bot: notifications, deal evaluations, email drafts, calendar updates, and more.

You own the bot. Kairos just powers it.

## Setting up your bot

For the full step-by-step setup, see the [Quick Start guide](/introduction/quick-start). Here's the short version:

1. Create a bot through [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) in Telegram
2. Copy the bot token and paste it in your Kairos dashboard
3. Click **Launch Your Bot** to start your sandbox
4. Send a message to your bot, copy the pair code, and paste it back in the dashboard

## What you can do in Telegram

Your bot is the single interface for everything Kairos offers:

- **Receive notifications** about new emails, brand deals, schedule updates, and payment reminders
- **Tap action buttons** to evaluate deals, reply to emails, create events, and more
- **Send messages** in natural language to ask questions, give instructions, or describe deals
- **Forward messages** from other chats for Kairos to process
- **Send screenshots** of DMs, profiles, contracts, or anything you want Kairos to read
- **Set preferences and rules** just by telling your bot what you want

## Security

Your bot is tied to your Kairos account through a verified pairing process. Only the bot owner can interact with Kairos, even if the bot is added to a group chat. Every action is verified against your user ID.

## Disconnecting

If you want to stop using Kairos, go to your Kairos dashboard and delete the bot. This removes your sandbox environment and all associated data.

You can also revoke the bot token through BotFather. If you later create a new bot and pair it again, it will be a completely fresh environment with no previous data.
