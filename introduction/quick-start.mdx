## Overview

Getting started with Kairos takes about 5 minutes. You'll create an account, connect your services, set up your messaging platform (Telegram or LINE), and start chatting.

## Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [kairos.ac](https://kairos.ac)
2. Sign up with your email or Google account
3. You'll land on the dashboard where you can set up your connections

## Step 2: Connect your services (optional)

The first thing you'll see is the option to connect Google and Notion. You can connect them now or skip and do it later from your dashboard.

<Info>
  **Every connection is optional.** Many features work without Gmail or Calendar connected. You can always connect, disconnect, or reconnect services from your dashboard at any time.
</Info>

### Google

Connecting Google lets Kairos monitor your inbox for brand deals and schedule-related emails, send replies on your behalf, and create calendar events.

1. Click **Connect Google**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Review the permissions and click **Continue**

**What permissions does Kairos request?**

| Permission | What it does |
|---|---|
| Read emails (gmail.readonly) | Your AI manager reads new emails to classify and notify you about brand deals, schedule items, and important messages. |
| Send emails (gmail.send) | Your AI manager drafts replies for you. No email is ever sent without your confirmation. |
| Calendar events (calendar.events) | Your AI manager creates and edits calendar events. It cannot delete events. |
| Basic identity (openid, email) | Identifies your account. No access to Drive, Photos, or other Google services. |

<Info>
  **Don't want to connect Google?** That's fine. You can forward emails to your agent as screenshots or text, describe brand deals directly, and create calendar events by telling your agent what to schedule.
</Info>

### Notion

Notion gives your AI manager a place to organize brand deals, store evaluation reports, and track payments.

1. Click **Connect Notion**
2. Select the Notion workspace you want to use
3. Grant access to the pages Kairos needs

Your agent will help you set up a brand deals database the first time you evaluate a deal. If you already have one, it can adapt to your existing structure.

## Step 3: Set up your messaging platform

Your AI manager lives inside a messaging bot that you create and own. Choose Telegram or LINE to get started.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Telegram and find @BotFather">
        Search for `@BotFather` in Telegram. This is Telegram's official tool for creating bots.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create a new bot">
        Send `/newbot` to BotFather. Follow the prompts to choose a name and username for your bot.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy the bot token">
        BotFather will give you an API token. Copy it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the token in Kairos">
        Paste the bot token in your Kairos dashboard. Kairos will verify that the token is valid.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch your bot">
        Click **Launch Your Bot**. This starts your private sandbox environment.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Need a visual guide? Watch how to [create your bot and get the token](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/UHcPXUv8d7s).
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="LINE">
    Kairos also works through LINE. To set up LINE:

    1. Go to your Kairos dashboard
    2. Select LINE as your messaging platform
    3. Scan the QR code to add the Kairos LINE official account
    4. Enter the pairing code shown in your dashboard
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Pair your bot

Once your sandbox is running, pair your messaging bot with your Kairos account.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your bot in Telegram">
        Find the bot you just created and send it any message.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy the pair code">
        The bot will reply with a **pair code** and your **UID**. Copy the entire message.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Paste in Kairos">
        Go back to your Kairos dashboard and paste the message to complete pairing.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Watch how to [pair your bot](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/gHjVSXQJGvI).
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="LINE">
    If you followed the LINE setup in Step 3, pairing is already done. Just enter the pairing code from your dashboard and you're good to go.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 5: Start using Kairos

You're all set! Open your messaging bot and say hello. Here are some things you can try right away:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Describe a brand deal" icon="handshake">
    "I got an offer from Nike for 2 Instagram reels, $3,000"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create a calendar event" icon="calendar-plus">
    "Schedule a shoot next Tuesday at 2 PM in downtown LA"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Draft an email" icon="pen">
    "Help me write a follow-up email to the brand I talked to last week"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Research a brand" icon="magnifying-glass">
    "Can you look into FitBrand Co? I want to know if they're legit"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage a client" icon="users">
    "Add a new client: Tanaka Corp, tax filing due April 15"
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You can also forward screenshots, share email content, or just chat naturally. Your AI manager understands context and will figure out what you need.
