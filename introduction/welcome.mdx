## Every creator deserves a manager

Running brand deals, answering emails, tracking payments, managing your calendar. These tasks pile up fast, and they pull you away from the work that matters: creating.

Kairos is an AI manager that lives in your Telegram. It handles the business side of being a creator, so you can spend your time on what you do best.

  <Card title="Manages brand deals" icon="handshake">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Handles your email" icon="envelope">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Organizes your schedule" icon="calendar">

## How it works

You interact with Kairos through a Telegram bot that you create and own. Connect the services you want, launch your bot, and your AI manager starts working for you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [kairos.ac](https://kairos.ac).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your services (optional)">
    Link Gmail, Google Calendar, and Notion. Each connection is optional — skip what you don't need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your Telegram bot">
    Create a bot through Telegram's BotFather, enter the token, and launch your private sandbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair and start chatting">
    Pair your bot with your Kairos account, then start talking to your AI manager. Send messages, forward emails, share screenshots — it understands them all.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use it your way

Kairos is flexible by design. You don't have to connect everything or use every feature.

- **Don't want to connect Gmail?** No problem. You can forward emails or describe deals directly to your agent.
- **Only need calendar management?** Just use that. Skip brand deals entirely.
- **Don't like a feature?** Tell your agent, or just ignore it. It learns from your choices.

There's no rigid workflow to follow. Use the parts that help you, skip the rest.

## Your data stays safe

We know you're trusting us with sensitive information. We take that seriously.

- All credentials are encrypted with bank-grade encryption (AES-256-GCM)
- Your emails are never stored in our database
- Every user gets a completely isolated private environment
- You can disconnect any service at any time. Deleting your bot from the Kairos dashboard removes your sandbox and all associated data. Our servers don't store your personal sensitive data
