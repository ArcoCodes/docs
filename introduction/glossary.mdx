---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Key terms used throughout the Kairos documentation."
---

### AI Manager

Your personal AI assistant powered by Kairos. It runs inside your Telegram bot and handles tasks like brand deal evaluation, email drafting, scheduling, and more.

### BotFather

Telegram's official tool for creating bots. You use it to create your personal Telegram bot, which becomes the interface for your AI manager. Open it at [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather).

### Brand Deal Lifecycle

The full journey of a brand collaboration: detection, evaluation, negotiation, contract review, production, delivery, and payment. Kairos can help at any stage.

### Creator Profile

A snapshot of your social media presence that Kairos uses to assess brand fit and suggest pricing. It includes your niche, engagement rates, content style, audience size, and more.

### Deep Research

A thorough brand investigation mode that covers company background, funding, reputation, pricing, social presence, and creator collaboration history. Best for high-value deals or unfamiliar brands.

### Fit Assessment

An evaluation of how well a brand aligns with your content, audience, and values. Rated as High, Medium, or Low fit.

### Pair Code

A code your Telegram bot sends when you message it for the first time. You paste this code into your Kairos dashboard to link the bot to your account.

### Sandbox

Your private, isolated environment where your AI manager runs. Each user gets their own sandbox. Your data, credentials, and preferences are completely separated from other users. Deleting your bot from the dashboard removes your sandbox and everything in it.

### Scheduled Task

A recurring job that Kairos runs automatically on a schedule you define. Examples include morning schedule summaries, evening previews, and overdue payment checks.

### Standard Research

A quick brand investigation mode covering company identity, product reputation, and creator track record. Best for smaller deals or familiar industries.
