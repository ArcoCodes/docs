## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Kairos?">
    Kairos is an AI manager that handles your business tasks — emails, scheduling, deals, client management — so you can focus on your real work and earn more. Whether you're a content creator, freelancer, or professional, Kairos takes care of the business side. You interact with it through a messaging bot that you own.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Kairos free?">
    Kairos is currently in early access through a waitlist. Pricing details will be shared as we get closer to general availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to connect all services (Gmail, Calendar, Notion) to use Kairos?">
    No. Every connection is optional. You can start with just a Telegram bot and add services later. Many creators only use one or two integrations and that works perfectly fine.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I don't connect Gmail?">
    You can still use most features. Forward emails as screenshots or text, describe brand deals in conversation, and create calendar events by telling your bot. Gmail just adds automatic email monitoring on top.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Messaging Platform

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why does Kairos use Telegram and LINE?">
    We believe in a lightweight approach — instead of building yet another app you'd have to download, Kairos meets you where you already are. Telegram and LINE are both fast, reliable, and support rich interactions like inline buttons, file sharing, and message forwarding. No new app to install, no new interface to learn.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use LINE instead of Telegram?">
    Yes! Kairos supports both LINE and Telegram. Choose whichever you prefer during setup. LINE is particularly popular with users in Japan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my Telegram bot secure? Can others access it?">
    Only the bot owner (you) can interact with your AI manager. Kairos verifies your identity through your Telegram user ID on every action. Even if your bot is added to a group chat, only you can use it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I delete my bot?">
    To properly remove your data, delete the bot from your Kairos dashboard. This removes your sandbox environment and all associated data. If you revoke the bot token through BotFather and later create a new one, it will be a completely fresh environment with no previous data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Brand Deals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can Kairos handle deals that come through DMs instead of email?">
    Yes. Screenshot the DM, forward the message, or just describe the deal in your own words. Kairos processes all of these the same way and can run the full evaluation workflow from any input method.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will Kairos send emails without my permission?">
    Never. Every email is drafted and shown to you first. You review, edit if needed, and explicitly approve before anything is sent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Kairos suggest pricing for brand deals?">
    Kairos considers your creator profile, platform benchmarks, the brand's stated budget, and deal-specific factors like usage rights and exclusivity. It suggests a range, not a fixed number. Over time, it also learns from your past pricing decisions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Kairos store my emails?">
    No. When Gmail notifies Kairos about a new email, it only sends an ID number. Your AI manager reads the email directly from Gmail inside your private environment. Email subjects, body text, attachments, and sender information never touch our database.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can see my data?">
    Only you. Every user gets a completely isolated sandbox environment. Your data, credentials, and AI manager state are never shared with other users, brands, or third parties.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account and data?">
    Go to your Kairos dashboard and delete your bot. This removes your sandbox environment and all associated data. Our servers don't store your personal sensitive data beyond what's needed to run your sandbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my data be used to train AI models?">
    No. Kairos does not use your data to train any AI models. Your data stays in your private sandbox and is only used to power your own AI manager.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What AI model does Kairos use?">
    Kairos currently uses MiMo-V2-Omni by Xiaomi as its primary model. You can review their [User Agreement](https://platform.xiaomimimo.com/#/docs/terms/user-agreement) and [Privacy Policy](https://platform.xiaomimimo.com/#/docs/terms/privacy-policy). We regularly evaluate models and may update them to give you the best performance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Kairos

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What languages does Kairos support?">
    Kairos understands and responds in any language. Email classification, brand research, and conversation all work regardless of the language you or the brand uses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Kairos for things other than brand deals?">
    Absolutely. You can use it to manage clients, schedule meetings, draft any email, search the web, set up reminders, organize your Notion workspace, and more. Brand deals are a core use case for creators, but Kairos is a general-purpose AI manager. Freelancers use it to track client projects, professionals use it to draft emails and manage their calendar — it adapts to however you work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Kairos get better over time?">
    Yes. Kairos learns your preferences from how you use it: your brand choices, communication style, pricing approach, and notification preferences. You can also set explicit rules at any time. The more you use it, the less you have to repeat yourself.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
