## The scene

You wake up, grab your phone, and open Telegram. There's a message from your AI manager waiting for you:

> **3 events today:**
> - 10:00 AM · Call with editor
> - 2:00 PM · Brand shoot (Nike campaign)
> - 5:00 PM · Content review deadline
>
> **1 overdue payment:**
> - FitBrand Co, 5 days overdue ($3,000)

No digging through calendars. No checking Notion. Everything you need to know, right there.

## What happens

You set up a morning briefing as a scheduled task. Kairos sends it at whatever time you choose. It pulls your calendar events for the day, checks for overdue payments, and puts it all in one clean message.

You can also add an evening preview that shows tomorrow's schedule, so you can plan ahead.

Both are completely optional. You choose the timing, the content, and whether you want them at all.

## Make it yours

The briefing is customizable. Want it at 7 AM instead of 10? Done. Want to skip weekends? Just say so. Only care about brand deal deadlines? Tell Kairos what to include.

> _"Send me a morning summary at 8 AM on weekdays, just calendar events and overdue payments."_

That's all it takes.
