## The scene

You get 4 brand emails in one afternoon. One is a collaboration invite. One is a follow-up on pricing. One is asking for your media kit. One is confirming a shoot date.

Each one needs a different reply, and you really don't want to spend the next hour in Gmail composing responses.

## What happens

Kairos reads each email and sends you a summary in Telegram. For each one, you can tap **Reply** and get a draft instantly.

The drafts aren't canned templates. They pull in context from your deal history, your preferences, and the email thread. The media kit request gets a short, professional response. The pricing follow-up references your earlier conversation. The shoot confirmation includes the dates from your calendar.

You review each draft, maybe tweak a word or two, and send. Four emails handled in a few minutes, all from Telegram.

## Why this matters

Email is one of those things that feels small but eats up hours. Especially when every reply needs to sound professional and reference the right details. Kairos gives you the first draft so you're just editing and approving instead of writing from scratch.
