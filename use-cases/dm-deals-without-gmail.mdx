## The scene

A brand slides into your Instagram DMs with a collab offer. You're interested, but everything in your workflow starts with email. Do you ask them to email you? Do you just wing it?

Neither. You screenshot the DM and send it to your Kairos bot.

## What happens

Kairos reads the screenshot, pulls out the brand name, what they're offering, and any details about compensation or deliverables.

From there, it works exactly like an email deal. You can evaluate the brand, get a fit assessment, and even draft a reply (which you copy back into the DM, or ask the brand to move to email).

The deal shows up in your Notion dashboard alongside all your other deals. Same tracking, same workflow.

## Works with any platform

This isn't limited to Instagram. You can forward or screenshot deals from X, TikTok, Telegram, WhatsApp, or literally anywhere. If you can send it to your bot, Kairos can work with it.

You can also just describe the deal in your own words:

> _"Hey, I got an offer from SportsBrand for 2 YouTube shorts at $2,500. Can you look into them?"_

That's enough to get started.
