## The scene

You just confirmed two brand deals in the same week. One needs content by Friday, the other by next Monday. You also have a dentist appointment on Wednesday and a call with your editor on Thursday. Things are getting tight.

## What happens

As your deals move through negotiation, Kairos suggests production milestones: script review, filming, editing, final delivery. It checks your Google Calendar before proposing any dates, so conflicts are caught before they happen.

When it spots an overlap, you get a clear choice: move the event, keep both, or skip the new one.

Once you confirm the schedule, Kairos creates the calendar events and syncs the milestones to your Notion deal pages. Everything stays in one place.

If something shifts (the brand pushes the deadline, or you need an extra day for editing), just tell your bot. It adjusts the calendar and updates Notion.

## Why this matters

When you're juggling multiple projects, it's easy to double-book yourself or miss a deadline. Kairos keeps your calendar and your deal pipeline in sync, so you always know what's coming and when.
