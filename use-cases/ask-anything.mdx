## The scene

You're negotiating a deal and the brand offers $1,500 for a 60-second Instagram Reel. Is that a good rate? What are other creators in your niche charging? You don't have time to Google it and sift through blog posts.

So you just ask your bot.

## What happens

> _"What's the average rate for a 60-second Instagram Reel in the fitness niche?"_

Kairos searches the web and comes back with a concise answer based on current data. Not a list of links. An actual answer you can use.

## More things you can ask

- _"When is VidCon this year?"_
- _"What are the FTC rules for sponsored content?"_
- _"What's the difference between exclusive and non-exclusive usage rights?"_
- _"Find me some good media kit templates"_
- _"What's going on with [brand name] lately?"_

It works for anything you'd normally search for, but faster because you never leave Telegram and the answer is already summarized for you.

## Why this matters

Small questions come up all the time when you're running your creator business. Having a research assistant in your pocket means you get answers immediately, right in the middle of whatever you're working on. No tab switching, no rabbit holes.
