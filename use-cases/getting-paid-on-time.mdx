## The scene

You delivered the content two weeks ago. The contract says Net 30, but day 35 comes and goes with no payment. You know you should follow up, but writing a "hey, where's my money?" email feels awkward. So you put it off. Another week passes.

Sound familiar?

## What happens

When you deliver content through Kairos, it records the payment due date and deal value in Notion. If you enable the payment tracker, Kairos checks for overdue payments every day.

The moment a payment is late, you get a notification: the brand name, how many days overdue, and the amount.

Tap **Send Reminder**, and Kairos drafts a professional follow-up email. Not aggressive, not passive. Just clear and firm, referencing the original terms and invoice.

If the brand still doesn't pay, subsequent reminders get progressively more direct. You review each one before it goes out.

When the payment finally hits, Kairos updates the deal status and saves the receipt.

## Why this matters

Chasing money is one of the most stressful parts of being a creator. It's also one of the easiest things to procrastinate on. Kairos takes the awkwardness out of it by handling the follow-up with a professional tone, so you stay on good terms with the brand while making sure you get what you're owed.
