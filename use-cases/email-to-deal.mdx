## The scene

It's Tuesday morning. You're editing a video when a new email lands in your inbox: a skincare brand wants to sponsor your next post. Sounds interesting, but you don't have time to research them, figure out your rate, or draft a reply right now.

So you don't. Kairos handles it.

## What happens

Your AI manager picks up the email, reads it, and sends you a Telegram notification with a quick summary: the brand name, what they want, and the budget they mentioned.

You tap **Evaluate**.

A few minutes later, you get a full report: the brand checks out, their products have solid reviews, and the deal is a good fit for your audience. Kairos suggests a rate range based on your profile and past deals.

You tap **Interested**. Kairos drafts a reply with your rate and availability. You tweak one line, hit **Send**, and go back to editing.

Over the next week, the negotiation plays out in email. Kairos drafts each reply, tracks the conversation in Notion, and creates calendar events for your shoot dates. When the contract arrives, it highlights the key terms so you know what to push back on.

After delivery, Kairos sets a payment reminder. When the payment is late, it drafts a polite follow-up. You get paid.

## Why this matters

The whole deal happened in the background of your creative work. You made decisions, but Kairos did the legwork. No spreadsheets, no forgotten follow-ups, no context switching.
