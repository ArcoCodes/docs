## April 2, 2026

- **Disconnect Google and Notion from dashboard**: You can now disconnect your Google and Notion accounts directly from the Kairos dashboard.

## April 1, 2026

- **Fixed Google login on mobile Safari**: Tapping "Sign in with Google" on Safari (iPhone/iPad) could occasionally get stuck loading. This has been fixed. Thanks to [hayashimon1](https://x.com/hayashimon1) for reporting this issue.
- **Fixed OAuth connections on mobile Safari**: Connecting Gmail, Notion, and Chatwork on Safari mobile browsers could occasionally fail. This has been fixed.

## March 31, 2026

- **Chatwork integration**: Connect your Chatwork account to monitor mentions across your team rooms. Kairos notifies you and helps you draft email replies or update Notion based on the mention content.

## March 27, 2026

- **LINE messaging support**: You can now use Kairos through LINE in addition to Telegram. Choose your preferred platform during setup.

## March 25, 2026

- **MiMo-V2-Omni as default AI model**: Switched to Xiaomi's MiMo-V2-Omni as the primary AI model, an omni-modal foundation model with stronger understanding and reasoning capabilities.
- **Fixed inconsistent reply language**: Kairos no longer randomly switches to English in the middle of a conversation when your preferred language is set to something else. Special thanks to [takuya@遊びが人生を救う](https://x.com/GpZTD8wcQW2o5u1) for reporting this issue.

## March 19, 2026

- **Profile analytics synced to Notion**: Your social media stats are now automatically saved to Notion after each profile analysis. Track your follower growth, engagement rates, and content quality over time across all connected platforms.

## March 18, 2026

- **Smarter brand deal evaluation**: Improved fit assessment with richer evaluation dimensions and upgraded pricing suggestions.
- **Brand research defaults to Standard mode**: Evaluations now automatically run Standard research first, with an option to go deeper afterward.
- **Automatic language detection**: Kairos now detects your preferred language during onboarding and uses it for all future notifications and replies.
- **TikTok connection**: You can now connect your TikTok account to Kairos.
- **Dashboard UI improvements**: Refreshed dashboard layout for a cleaner experience.
- **Docs now available in Chinese and Japanese**: Full documentation is now available in 中文 and 日本語, with a language switcher on every page.
- **Fixed evaluation report visibility**: Evaluation reports now appear as a new message at the bottom of your chat, instead of editing an old message that may have scrolled off screen.
- **Fixed button conflicts**: Buttons no longer override each other when multiple notifications arrive at the same time.

## March 17, 2026

- **Fixed email price formatting**: Prices in email drafts (like $600) are no longer broken when sent through Kairos. Previously, dollar signs could be stripped from outgoing emails.
- **Fixed email notification filtering**: Your notification preferences (like "only notify me about sponsorships") were not being applied correctly during email triage, causing unwanted notifications. This is now fixed — your saved preferences are properly read and enforced.

Special thanks to [MounirTech](https://www.youtube.com/@MounirTech) for reporting both issues during alpha testing.

## March 12, 2026

- **Platform update**: Updated core AI engine to the latest version for improved performance and reliability.

## March 9, 2026

- **Kimi as default AI model**: Switched to Kimi 2.5 as the primary AI model for faster and more accurate responses.
- **Modular Notion integration**: Improved Notion skill architecture for more reliable database operations and faster deal tracking updates.

## March 8, 2026

- **Skills hot-update**: Your AI manager now receives skill improvements automatically without any downtime or action needed from you.
- **Security patch**: Updated dependencies to address a security vulnerability.

## March 7, 2026

- **Improved button interactions**: Telegram inline buttons now work more reliably with better context restoration when you return to a conversation after a break.

## March 6, 2026

- **Deep brand research**: When evaluating a brand deal, you can now choose between a quick check (Standard) or a thorough investigation (Deep) that covers company background, funding, reputation, pricing, and creator collaboration history.

## March 5, 2026

- **Smart email classification**: Emails are now classified in two stages. Your AI manager first checks the sender and subject before reading the full email, so irrelevant messages are filtered out faster without wasting processing time.
- **Interactive profile setup**: Instead of requiring specific screenshot formats, your AI manager now guides you step by step through providing your social media information.
- **Screenshot-based profile analysis**: Can't share a URL? Send a screenshot of your social media profile and your AI manager will extract your stats automatically.
- **Automated daily reminders**: Your AI manager can now send you morning schedule summaries and evening previews through scheduled tasks.
- **Profile history**: Your creator profile snapshots are saved over time, so you and your AI manager can track your growth trends.

## March 4, 2026

- **Full brand deal lifecycle**: Your AI manager now supports the complete brand deal workflow, from initial detection through evaluation, negotiation, contract review, production tracking, delivery, and payment collection.
- **Streaming responses**: Your AI manager now sends responses progressively in Telegram, so you see text appearing in real time instead of waiting for the full message.

## March 3, 2026

- **Scheduled tasks**: You can now ask your AI manager to create recurring tasks, like daily schedule reminders, payment due date checks, or weekly summaries. Fully customizable timing and content.
- **Gmail integration**: Your AI manager can now monitor your inbox in real time, read emails, and send replies on your behalf (with your approval).
- **Google Calendar integration**: Create, view, and manage calendar events directly through your AI manager.
- **Notion integration**: Track brand deals, store evaluation reports, and manage your workflow in Notion. Your AI manager handles the database setup and updates.

## March 2, 2026

- **Launch**: Kairos is live! Initial release with core AI manager capabilities, including web search, brand research, social media profile analysis, and creator productivity tools, all accessible through your personal Telegram bot.
